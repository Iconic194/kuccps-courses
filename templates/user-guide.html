Welcome to the KUCCPS Course Qualification System

This system helps Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE) graduates to identify courses they qualify for in universities, colleges, and technical training institutions based on their exam results.

Follow this step-by-step guide to understand how to use the system effectively and make informed decisions about your future education.

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Select Your Category and Enter Grades

Begin by selecting the category that matches your educational goals:

Degree Programs Diploma Programs Certificate Programs KMTC Courses Artisan Courses
For Degree Programs:
  • Enter your KCSE grades for all relevant subjects
  • Enter your Cluster Points for all 20 clusters
  • Cluster points determine your eligibility for specific degree programs
For Other Categories (Diploma, Certificate, KMTC, Artisan):
  • Enter your KCSE grades for all relevant subjects
  • No cluster points required for these categories
Tip: You can check multiple categories, but each additional category beyond the first will incur an extra charge.
2

Provide Email and Index Number

After entering your grades, you'll be prompted to provide:

  • Email Address: Used to send your results and for account recovery
  • KCSE Index Number: Your unique examination identification number
Note: Ensure your email address is active and correctly entered to receive your results.
3

Payment Process

The system uses a tiered payment structure:

Payment Scenario Amount Description
First-time User KSH 200 Initial payment for your first category check
Additional Categories KSH 100 per category If you want to check courses in multiple categories (e.g., Degree + Diploma)
Payment Instructions:
  1. Select your payment method: M-Pesa or Airtel Money
  2. Enter your phone number registered with your mobile money service
  3. You will receive a payment prompt on your phone
  4. Enter your mobile money PIN to complete the transaction
  5. Wait for confirmation before proceeding
Payment Example: If you check Degree courses (KSH 200) and also want to check Diploma courses (additional KSH 100), your total payment will be KSH 300.
4

Viewing Your Results

After successful payment, your results will be displayed showing:

  • All courses you qualify for based on your grades
  • Institutions offering each course
  • Programme codes for easy KUCCPS application
Using the Course Basket:
  • Add courses you're interested in to your basket for easy retrieval
  • Your basket saves your selections for future reference
  • You can review your basket before making final application decisions
Tip: Use the programme codes from your results when applying through the official KUCCPS portal to ensure accurate application.
5

Retrieving Previous Results

If you've already made a payment and want to retrieve your results again:

  1. Go to the results retrieval section
  2. Enter your MPesa receipt number from your payment
  3. Enter your KCSE index number
  4. Submit to view your previously generated results
Note: Keep your payment receipt safe as it's required for result retrieval.

Additional Information

Understanding Cluster Points

Cluster points are specific to degree programs and are calculated based on:

  • Your performance in specific subject combinations
  • Subject weights assigned to each degree program
  • There are 20 different clusters for various degree programs

You can find your cluster points on your KCSE result slip or through the KNEC portal.

Troubleshooting

Payment issues?

  • Ensure you have sufficient balance in your mobile money account
  • Check that you've entered the correct phone number
  • Wait for payment confirmation before proceeding

Can't view results?

  • Verify your index number is correctly entered
  • Ensure payment was successfully completed
  • Use the retrieval option with your receipt number

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I check courses for multiple categories at once?

Yes, you can select multiple categories (e.g., Degree, Diploma, and Certificate). The first category costs KSH 200, and each additional category costs KSH 100.

What if I don't have my cluster points?

Cluster points are only required for degree programs. If you don't have them, you can still check diploma, certificate, KMTC, and artisan courses using just your KCSE grades.

How long does it take to get results after payment?

Results are typically generated immediately after successful payment. If you experience delays, use the retrieval option with your payment receipt.

Can I change my course selections after viewing results?

Yes, you can add courses to your basket and review them later. However, to check different categories, you may need to make additional payments.

What if I lose my payment receipt?

Your payment receipt is important for retrieving results. If lost, contact customer support with details of your transaction (phone number, approximate time of payment) for assistance.
